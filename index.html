Dr. Nikhilanand Arya

Assistant Professor @ Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahahbad (IIIT-A), Prayagraj, UP, India, 201105
Research Publications Teaching Contact

My research area includes application of multi-modal machine learning and deep learning in computational biology and healthcare domain. I hold a PhD in Computer Science & Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Patna (2019–2023). My existing research focuses on multi-modal deep learning methods for cancer prognosis and medical image classification. I develop architectures that combine multi-omics, clinical, and histopathology data to predict cancer survival and build robust, interpretable models for real-world healthcare problems.

Research Interests

Interests: Computational Biology, Multi-modal Deep Learning, Cancer Prognosis, Histopathology & Dermoscopy Image Analysis, Generative Models for Missing Data, Graph Neural Networks, Fuzzy Ensembles.

  • Multi-modal Breast-Cancer Survival Prognosis — deep architectures using six TCGA modalities for robust survival prediction.
  • GIMPP — generative models to impute missing modalities for prognosis prediction.
  • Graph-based Prognosis Models — GCN + contrastive learning for breast cancer survival prediction.
  • Skin Lesion Classification — EfficientNet ensembles with fuzzy ensemble strategies for dermoscopy images.
